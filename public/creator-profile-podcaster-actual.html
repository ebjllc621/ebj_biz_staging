About

I'm Marcus Chen, host and executive producer of The Growth Mindset Show — one of the top-ranked business and entrepreneurship podcasts with over 1.8 million downloads and 312 episodes published since 2022. Every week I sit down with world-class founders, investors, and thinkers to extract the mindset frameworks and tactical strategies that drive extraordinary results. Past guests include executives from Fortune 500 companies, unicorn startup founders, and bestselling authors. My mission is to make elite-level thinking accessible to ambitious professionals and entrepreneurs worldwide. I'm open to sponsorships, guest collaborations, and live event partnerships that align with my audience's values around growth, business, and personal development.

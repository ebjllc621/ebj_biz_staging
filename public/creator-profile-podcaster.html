About the Show

The Growth Mindset Show is a weekly deep-dive into the minds of the world's most interesting founders, creators, and unconventional thinkers. Now in its 6th year, the show has featured over 300 conversations exploring what it really takes to build something meaningful.

Each episode goes beyond the highlight reel — we explore the failures, the pivots, the 3am decisions that nobody talks about. Whether you're a first-time founder or a seasoned operator, you'll walk away with actionable insights and a fresh perspective.
